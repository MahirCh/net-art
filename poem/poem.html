First you will need:

  • One jar
  • One cup of room temperature water
  • One lemon
  • Ice
  • Sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • A fridge
  1. First, pour the cup of water into the jar
  2. Next, cut one lemon in half and squeeze it into the jar
  3. After this, mix it well with a spoon
  4. Then, add a small amount of salt as well as one spoon of sugar
  5. Mix again and add ice
  6. Finally, put it in the fridge to make cold and use later as a refreshment!
